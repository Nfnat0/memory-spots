Mind Palace

Turn place photos into memory routes.

Mind Palace is an iPhone app for placing sticky notes on familiar landmark photos and reviewing memories by theme.

View Support Privacy Policy

What You Can Do

Create Photo Routes

Arrange photos of memorable places, such as your room, desk, commute, or favorite park.

Switch Themes

Use the same photo route for exams, shopping lists, presentation prep, or any topic you want to remember.

Review Sticky Notes

Walk through the notes placed on your photos and check what you remember.
