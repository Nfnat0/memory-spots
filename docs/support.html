Support

If you need help using Mind Palace, please check the notes below.

I cannot add photos

Open the iPhone Settings app and confirm that Mind Palace has permission to use Photos or Camera. Photos are stored in the app's local storage and are not sent externally.

Photos do not appear on the map

Photos without a location are not shown on the map. You can set a location later from a photo in an album.

I cannot see notes for a theme

Notes are separated by theme. On the album detail screen, confirm that the theme you want to view is selected.
