Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Mind Palace is an app for placing notes on landmark photos and reviewing memories. This policy explains how Mind Palace handles data.

Information We Collect

Mind Palace does not use account registration, advertising, analytics services, or external server data transmission. Albums, themes, notes, review results, added photos, and photo locations created in the app are stored on the user's device.

Photos, Camera, and Location

The photo library is used to add user-selected photos to memory routes. The camera is used to take landmark photos. Location is used to show photos on the map and to associate a captured photo with its place.

Third Parties

Mind Palace does not sell, share, or transmit user data to third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete albums, photos, themes, and notes in the app. Deleting the app from the device also deletes app data stored on the device.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us through GitHub Issues.
